Popular Destinations

Explore the spiritual heritage and natural wonders of Odisha, from ancient temples to serene sanctuaries.

Puri Jagannath Temple

The Puri Jagannath Temple, located in Odisha, is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a form of Lord Vishnu. It is renowned for its annual Rath Yatra, where the deities are taken out in grand chariots.

Konark Sun Temple

The Konark Sun Temple, located in Odisha, India, is a 13th-century architectural marvel built by King Narasimhadeva I. Designed as a colossal chariot of the Sun God Surya, it features intricately carved stone wheels, horses, and walls.

Dhauli Shanti Stupa

Dhauli Shanti Stupa, located near Bhubaneswar in Odisha, marks the site of the historic Kalinga War. Built with the help of Japanese Buddhists, it symbolizes peace and commemorates Ashoka's transformation to Buddhism.

Lingraj Temple

Lingaraj Temple, situated in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, is a majestic 11th-century temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It showcases the grandeur of Kalinga architecture and is one of the oldest and largest temples in the region.

Bhitarkanika

Bhitarkanika, located in Odisha, is a renowned national park and wildlife sanctuary known for its rich biodiversity and mangrove forests. It is home to the endangered saltwater crocodile, various bird species, and unique wetland ecosystems.
