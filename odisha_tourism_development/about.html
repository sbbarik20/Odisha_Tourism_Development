Odisha Tourism Development

Odisha Tourism Development is a dedicated initiative by the Government of Odisha aimed at promoting and enhancing tourism across the state. It works under the guidance of the Department of Tourism, Odisha, with a mission to highlight the state's rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, historical landmarks, and vibrant festivals.

Established to boost tourism infrastructure and attract both domestic and international tourists, Odisha Tourism Development undertakes various projects including heritage conservation, eco-tourism development, beach tourism, and pilgrimage promotion. It facilitates hospitality services, tourism training programs, and travel infrastructure upgrades, ensuring a seamless experience for visitors.

The organization also supports local communities through sustainable tourism practices and promotes Odisha as a must-visit destination through strategic campaigns and partnerships. It plays a key role in preserving tribal culture, showcasing classical dance forms like Odissi, and encouraging art and craft traditions such as Pattachitra, silver filigree, and applique work from Pipili.

Odisha Tourism Development actively collaborates with national and international agencies to improve tourism services and attract investments in the hospitality and leisure sectors.
